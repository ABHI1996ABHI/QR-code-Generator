Cookies Policy

How The QuickQR Uses Cookies and Tracking Technologies

Last Updated: June 14, 2025

Quick Summary: We use cookies to make our QR code generation service work better for you. This includes remembering your preferences, keeping you logged in, and understanding how our service is used to make improvements.

1. What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files that are stored on your device (computer, tablet, or mobile) when you visit our website. They help us remember information about your visit, which can make it easier to visit our site again and make the service more useful to you.

Similar technologies include:

  • Web beacons: Small graphic images that help us analyze website usage
  • Local storage: Technology that stores data locally on your device
  • Session storage: Temporary storage that lasts only for your browser session
  • Pixels: Tiny images used for tracking and analytics

2. Why We Use Cookies

The QuickQR uses cookies for several important purposes:

  • Essential functionality: To make our QR code generator work properly
  • User authentication: To keep you logged in to your account
  • Preferences: To remember your settings and choices
  • Security: To protect against fraud and unauthorized access
  • Analytics: To understand how our service is used and improve it
  • Performance: To optimize loading times and user experience

4. Third-Party Cookies

Some cookies are placed by third-party services that appear on our pages. We use the following third-party services:

  • Google Analytics: For website analytics and user behavior insights
  • Google Fonts: For typography and font loading
  • Cloudflare: For content delivery and security
  • Facebook Pixel: For advertising and conversion tracking (if enabled)
  • Google Ads: For advertising measurement (if enabled)

These third parties may use cookies to collect information about your online activities across different websites over time.

5. How to Control Cookies

5.1 Browser Settings

Popular Browser Cookie Controls:

  • Chrome: Settings → Privacy and Security → Cookies and other site data
  • Firefox: Settings → Privacy & Security → Cookies and Site Data
  • Safari: Preferences → Privacy → Manage Website Data
  • Edge: Settings → Cookies and site permissions → Cookies and site data
  • Opera: Settings → Advanced → Privacy & Security → Site Settings

5.2 Our Cookie Preference Center

You can manage your cookie preferences directly on our website using our cookie preference center. This allows you to:

  • Accept or reject non-essential cookies
  • Change your preferences at any time
  • View detailed information about each cookie category
  • Withdraw consent for previously accepted cookies

5.3 Opt-Out Links

For specific third-party services, you can opt out directly:

  • Google Analytics: Google Analytics Opt-out
  • Google Ads: Google Ad Settings
  • Facebook: Facebook Ad Preferences

6. Impact of Disabling Cookies

Please Note: If you disable cookies, some features of The QuickQR may not work properly:

  • You may need to log in repeatedly
  • Your preferences won't be saved
  • Some QR code generation features may be limited
  • We won't be able to remember your settings

7. Mobile App Tracking

If you use our mobile app, we may use similar tracking technologies including:

  • Device identifiers: Unique identifiers for your mobile device

  • Push notification tokens: To send you relevant notifications

8. International Transfers

Some of our third-party cookie providers may transfer data internationally. We ensure that appropriate safeguards are in place, including:

9. Children's Privacy

Our service is not intended for children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect information from children through cookies. If we discover that we have collected such information, we will delete it immediately.
