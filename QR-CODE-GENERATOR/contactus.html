Frequently Asked Questions

How do I create a QR code?
Simply enter your URL, text, or other data in the input field, select your preferred format, and click "Generate QR Code". Your QR code will be created instantly and ready for download.
Are QR codes free to generate?
Yes! The QuickQR offers free QR code generation for personal use. Premium features and bulk generation options are available for business users.
What formats can I download?
You can download your QR codes in PNG, JPG, SVG, and PDF formats. Each format is optimized for different use cases - PNG for web, SVG for scalability, and PDF for printing.
Do QR codes expire?
Static QR codes (like URLs and text) never expire. However, the content they point to (like websites) might change. Dynamic QR codes in premium accounts can be updated without changing the code itself.
Is my data secure?
Absolutely. We use HTTPS encryption for all data transmission, and we don't store your QR code content permanently. Check our Privacy Policy for complete details on data handling.
Can I track QR code scans?
Scan tracking is available with our premium accounts. This feature provides insights into when, where, and how often your QR codes are scanned.
